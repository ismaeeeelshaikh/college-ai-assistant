=== ADMISSION PROCESS ===

APSIT offers admissions through two channels:
- 10% through Institute-level admissions
- 90% through Centralized Admission Process (CAP) by Government of Maharashtra

Admission Timeline:
Admissions generally begin after MHT-CET exam results and continue until the cutoff date given by CET CELL.

=== ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA ===

First Year Engineering (Direct Admission):
- Indian National
- Passed HSC or equivalent with Physics and Mathematics compulsory
- One of Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical/Vocational subjects
- Minimum 45% marks in above subjects combined (40% for Backward class/PwD candidates from Maharashtra)
- Valid MHT-CET or JEE Main score

Direct Second Year (Lateral Entry):
- Indian National
- Diploma in Engineering with minimum 45% marks (40% for Backward class/PwD)
- OR B.Sc. degree with minimum 45% marks (40% for Backward class/PwD) and HSC with Mathematics

Out-of-State Students:
- MHT-CET 2025/JEE Main score of current year
- XII examination in English with minimum 45% marks in PCM
- Registration on Maharashtra CET Cell portal is MANDATORY

=== ENTRANCE EXAMINATIONS ===

Mandatory Entrance Exams: MHT-CET or JEE Main (current year)
Registration: Maharashtra CET Cell portal registration is mandatory for all candidates

=== APPLICATION PROCESS ===

Institute Level Admission:
1. Register online on www.apsit.edu.in
2. Mandatory CAP registration through Scrutiny Centers
3. Collect hardcopy application form from college
4. Submit form with documents before deadline
5. Merit list published on website
6. Attend admission day with original documents and fees

CAP Admission:
1. Register on Maharashtra CET Cell portal
2. Fill option form selecting APSIT courses
3. Participate in CAP rounds
4. Report to allotted college if selected
